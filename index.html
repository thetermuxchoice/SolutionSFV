BHOUT Sensor Technology

The first bag with a brain.

BHOUT AI merges sensors on the bag with computer vision. Track your every move, improve your technique, avoid injuries. Have fun.

Bag AI processing unit

Connects all the BHOUT sensors and 3D computer vision, and turns them into unique data, like never before.

Computer vision

A personal coach watching your every-move. BHOUT computer vision identifies every punch, kick, elbow or knee strike on the bag. Stop guessing, start knowing.

Bag sensors

How strong are you? Multiple sensors along the body of the bag calculate the power of every strike. You focus on getting stronger. Let us worry about the data.

Docking station

A scale on the docking station guarantees that we keep track of the water in the core of the bag. To compete against yourself or others, first we need to level the playing field.

Strength Training

The bag that gets you stronger.

A boxing bag is just for striking? Think again! Easily rotate the bag 180º and access a new level of experience.

Anchor points.

What is the difference between the BHOUT bag and other boxing bags?
What is the difference between the BHOUT bag and other boxing bags?

The BHOUT Bag is a boxing bag unlike all the others. It’s multi-layered system guarantees the best impact feeling.

It is the worlds first bag with a brain (i.e. a processor) that combines the impact information from the sensors on the body of the bag, with ones in the docking station and the 3D computer vision form the camera, integrated by our Artificial Intelligence system. All together, it allows you to know what kind of strike you are delivering (punches, kicks, elbows, knees), how many times, where and how hard. And future software releases will allow for more data and information.

What is the BHOUT App and how much does it cost?
What is the BHOUT App and how much does it cost?

The BHOUT App allows you to create your own STRK routines or choose routines created by the community, play BHOUT and get stats like never before.

You can also join communities in your city, company, school, people of your level, etc. The BHOUT world is for everyone and if you can’t find your community, you can also create it from scratch.

The BHOUT App works on a subscription model. The price is €39.99 per user, per month. Billing only starts after the first 12 months have passed, which are our initial offer to all early customers.
